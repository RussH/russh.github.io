Open-source recruiting software

Applicant tracking that stays in your hands.

OpenCATS is a free, community-maintained applicant tracking system and recruiting CRM for teams that need a practical way to manage candidates, jobs, submissions, and hiring activity without vendor lock-in.

Why OpenCATS

Built for ownership, clarity, and everyday recruiting work.

Candidate management

Keep candidate records, resumes, notes, status history, and recruiting activity in one open system your team can operate and audit.

Recruiting CRM

Support staffing agencies, internal recruiting teams, and hiring workflows with jobs, companies, contacts, submissions, and follow-up activity.

Open-source control

Host OpenCATS yourself, inspect the code, contribute improvements, and keep your recruiting data under your own operational policies.

Community

Maintained in the open by users and contributors.

OpenCATS development happens on GitHub. Contributors help with code, testing, documentation, issue reports, design improvements, release feedback, and support for other users.
